# Medical Imaging Modalities

## Overview
Medical imaging allows visualization of internal body structures for diagnosis and treatment planning.

## Common Modalities

### X-ray
- Uses ionizing radiation
- Excellent for bone imaging and chest exams

### MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging)
- Uses strong magnetic fields
- Superior soft tissue contrast
- No ionizing radiation

### CT (Computed Tomography)
- Combines multiple X-ray images
- Provides 3D anatomical information
- Faster than MRI
